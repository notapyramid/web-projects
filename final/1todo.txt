1 use each of inline and internal styling

-Your website should include at least one alert and two onclick events. How and where you use them is up to you.
-Majority of your JS should live within your script file, but it is ok to have an alert within your HTML doc.

Home page titled index.html

    This page will be an overview of your topic.  You can start with an <h1>  and an image. Then use <ol> and/or <ul> to briefly describe which details the viewer can find on your topic in the rest of the website. 

About page titled about.html

    This page will go into more detail about your topic, and should include at least 250 words. Include at least one image on this page as well.

Gallery page titled gallery.html

    This page will be a gallery of images related to your topic. It should start with an <h1> saying what the page includes images of, and you should include at least 6 different images. Each image should include a short description (one or two sentences is fine) of the image. You may want to use <div's> to organize your image with it's caption.  

Contact page titled contact.html

    This page will include your contact information. You only need to include your EPS email address here, and it can be a fake one if you are worried about privacy. This page should also include an image.
